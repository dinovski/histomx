Diagnosis based scores represent the probability that a biopsy has an expression profile similar to reference samples with a given histology based diagnosis. Outcomes include the following: AMR, TCMR, NRKI (non-rejection kidney injury), and no rejection or injury diagnosis. The sample range is the confidence interval for the ensemble score. The Normal range is the middle 50% of scores in biopsies with no rejection or injury diagnosis.
